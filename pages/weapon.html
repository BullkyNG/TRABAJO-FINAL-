Weapon Type List:

    2. Heavy damaging weapon, slow with great poise damage, not a great moveset

  2. Greataxes:

    3. Heavy damaging weapon, slow with high poise damage and great recovery, good jumping attack

  3. Bows:

    4. Long distance weapon, really efective against casting opponents comes in 3 different types (Long - Medium - Short)

  4. Spears:

    5. Spears are really fast and do a lot of thrusting damage, they are better when power stanced
